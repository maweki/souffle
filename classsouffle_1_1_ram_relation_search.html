bool souffle::RamRelationSearch::pureExistenceCheck = false
private

Determines whether this search operation is merely verifying the existence of a value (e.g.

rel(_,_), rel(1,2), rel(1,_) or rel(X,Y) where X and Y are bound) or actually contributing new variable bindings (X or Y are not bound).

The exists-only can be check much more efficient than the other case.
